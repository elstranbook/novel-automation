Despite myself, I felt a spark of curiosity flicker. Maybe this wasn’t just a standoff after all.




## Chapter 4: Failed Attempts and Flickering Hopes


The door to the lighthouse’s back chamber groaned as I pushed it open, the faint scent of salt and mothballs wrapping around me like a dusty shroud. The dim light struggled through the stained glass, casting muted shards of amber and green onto the cracked wooden floor.
