## Chapter 17: The Phoenix’s Flight

"Together, we are forging a path from ashes," I continued, feeling the tremor of doubt in my own heart quiet beneath the fire of purpose. "The Phoenix's flame is not just a symbol—it is our pledge. To stand united, to protect one another, and to build from our shared strength. This peace is delicate, yes, but it is real. It is ours to guard."
